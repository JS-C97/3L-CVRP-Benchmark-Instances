# Benchmark Instances for 3L-CVRP and Its Variants

This repository centralizes the publicly available and active URLs for benchmark instances discussed in our survey paper to ensure long-term accessibility for the Operations Research (OR) community.

## 1. 3L-CVRP instances
* **Gendreau et al. (2006):** 27 Euclidean CVRP instances with 15–100 clients. 
  * **Link:** [https://site.unibo.it/operations-research/en](https://site.unibo.it/operations-research/en)
* **Tarantilis et al. (2009):** Three classes of instances (50–125 clients) with varying item dimensions.
  * **Link:** [http://www.msl.aueb.gr/management_science/networks.htm](http://www.msl.aueb.gr/management_science/networks.htm)

## 2. 3L-CVRPTW / 3L-PCVRPTW instances
* **Moura and Oliveira (2009):** 46 widely used instances with time windows.
  * **Link:** [http://www.apdio.pt/esicup](http://www.apdio.pt/esicup)
* **Bortfeldt and Homberger (2013):** Large-scale instances (100–1000 clients).
  * **Link:** [http://www.fernuni-hagen.de/evis](http://www.fernuni-hagen.de/evis)
* **Krebs et al. (2021):** 600 instances considering item fragility and load-bearing.
  * **Link:** [https://doi.org/10.24352/UB.OVGU-2020-139](https://doi.org/10.24352/UB.OVGU-2020-139)

## 3. 3L-HFVRP instances
* **Wei et al. (2014a):** 20–100 clients with multiple vehicle types.
  * **Link:** [http://www.computational-logistics.org/orlib/3l-hfvrp](http://www.computational-logistics.org/orlib/3l-hfvrp)

*(Note: Please refer to our manuscript for detailed descriptions of each dataset.)*
